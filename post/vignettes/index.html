A collection of original and flagrantly stolen ideas that are currently or have recently taken up residence in the more organized parts of my conciousness.

Interfacing with the World Machine

A giant, globe spanning machine that behaves more like a biological organism than infrastructure. Resilent to changes in government, ecological disaster, and limited nuclear exchanges. The world machine occupies real estate in the closest reaches of space and the furthest depths of the ocean.

The dutiful technicians of the World Machine see it’s connectivity as a religious rite, rather than as a mere means of communication. After the final, deafening throes of globe-spanning war become a distant memory, these devout men and women will quietly, patiently, maintain humanity’s last great civilizational leap.

Short Horror Film Concept

Two art school friends, recently graduated, treat themselves to a VRBO stay at a cabin in upstate NY designed by a famous architect. After noticing small changes to each other’s behavior, they slowly realize that things are not what they seem. Basically, that post from McMansion Hell, but the property is malevolent instead of elitist.
