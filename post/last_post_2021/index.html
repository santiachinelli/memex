End of 2021: Reflections

December 31, 2021

As we head into 2022 (Minus Betty White, RIP) I’m thinking more and more about the movie, Aniara. I’m also moving to Boston in March, and besides all the work involved, I’m still feeling pretty hesitant about putting together a portfolio.

Since New Year’s is the traditional, permitted mass inflection point, I might as well take advantage.

  • I want to continue working out in the new year, although I think this holiday experiment in simplifing to a heavy kettlebell has been mostly disaster. Part of the problem might be a lack of discipline, although I had no issue sticking to a routine before. I wonder if an adjustable set of two would be a better investment.

  • I think I need to focus less on unfettered learning (last year’s Annual Theme was learning) and more on honing. 2022 will be my Year of Honing existing skills and habits.

  • I will pull together a CV and landing page that demonstrates my skillset(s) and value add. I will run that as an extension of off this memex, since it’s trivially easy to add another subdomain+GithubPages static site with Hugo. Damn, I love Hugo.

Existing Skills and Habits I Want To Hone

1). Cardio Routine 2). Strength Training 3). Javascript depth of understanding, as well as WASM 4). As an extension of JS, Solidity programming. 5). A-Frame and D3.js for interesting visualizations. 6). Deno deploy and ‘nocode’ deployment stacks like JAMstack and Netlify’s thing. 7). Automation with Ansible and similar tools. I need to have a one-man Continuous Integration flow.

2022: Year of Honing, Let’s go!
